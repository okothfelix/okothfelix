Project Description

Problem Statement

The client (Kenyatta University, Nairobi Kenya) has a challenge of lectures clashing in the timetable causing some students to miss or postpone their lectures. This affects both the lecturer and especially the students schedule as both have to miss out on a few classes to make the schedule align. The client needs a system which can generate the students timetables based on when the lectures had maximum attendance and ensure the lecture venues do not have more than one lecture.

Key Steps

This problem is solved by all the science and engineering departments therefore I make my contributions to the physics department. Through the use of machine learning this is how I have made progress in trying to solve the above challenge
i) The Unstructured Supplementary Service Data (USSD) application.
Through this application the students with feature phones would be able to mark attendance and be logged in as present during the lecture. The ussd application was developed in python and flask framework.
ii) The mobile application
The mobile app, students with smart-phones were able to mark attendance and be logged in as present during the lecturer. The mobile application was developed in Flutter for both the android and IOS operating systems.
iii) The Web application Portal
The web app portal allowed the administrators of the system to monitor how each class being attended and get the analytics of both the lecturers and students average attendance. It also provided an interface for scheduling and rescheduling of the classes as it was among the features the clients had requested.
iv) The IOT Device
The IOT device allowed for students who had a challenge of not having both a smart-phone or a feature phone to be able to log into the class. Students fingerprints were captured and stored in the database and while in class it was just a matter of confirmation of the prints.
