Services Details

Maarifa Education is a communication application for higher learning institutions that enable the management to monitor lecture attendance. Through the application the lecturer activates the attendance form which is online while in class by providing a class code that acts as a password for the lecturer. The application then captures the geolocation of the lecturer phone and sends out a notification in form of an SMS to the lecturer notifying him/her that the attendance form is online and the students can proceed to sign it from their end. The students through their accounts can now use the class code provided by the lecturer to sign the form.

The application verifies the class code and the geolocation of the student’s phone number before successfully adding their details to the attendance form. The lecturers can also get the attendance reports of the unit they teach with average attendance of each student computed up to the current point in the semester. This attendance report is sent to the lecturer’s email address as an excel sheet. The administrators can also view the same report in the administrator’s website portal and get analysis for the average attendance of the university based on the different categories i.e., campus, level, program, department which can be downloaded.

The application also includes student lecturer evaluation for the students which is linked with the attendance report to allow only students who have met the threshold attendance to evaluate the lecturer. The administrators can view the evaluation report for each unit and the responses analysis which they can use for decision making. The application was tested with the department of Microbiology and Parasitology School of Medicine, Kenyatta University.

How long does it take to setup.

Set up period takes 2-3 days where we load the timetables and the lecturers details. We also carry out a training session with the lecturers and instruct them on how to use the application.

How long does the pilot phase take.

Usually we pilot the application for one semester for a given school before setting up the application of the entire institution to ensure all features needed are working as required by the administration

How much does the licenses cost?

The license for installing the application to your institution cost from Kshs. 150,000 -2.5M depending on the number of campuses. We also charge a maintenance fee annually where we do the maintenance of the system three times a year.
