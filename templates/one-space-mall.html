Project Description

Problem Statement

The client wanted to create a solution targeting stores that had an online audience but could not afford to build one as most of the target market were the vendors using social media to sell their products i.e. TikTok. One of the specifications of the platform was that it should be able to handle buy now pay later as this was not possible with social media venues.

Key Steps

The project had two main ares of development.
i) The user interface
Through a web application it was possible for the users to shop easily, track orders and to check their next due payments for clients who had opted for lipa mdogo mdogo.
The merchants also had a portal access to where they would be able to process the orders received, follow up on the clients who had opted for pay later option, send out notification in form of SMS and newsletters and register affiliates to help them in the marketing of their products.
ii) The credit model
Since the application accepted the buy now pay later option there was need to develop a credit model to protect the merchants against risk of customers not being able to complete payments made.
Through machine learning I was able to design a model that enables to generate a credit score of the customer based on the initial deposit made on the product and the previous purchases made. The model was also able to generate payment plans based on the customer choice i.e. weekly, monthly and the amount to be paid.

Deliverables

The application development was successful. I was able to hand over the project to the client and was able to test the credit model through a partnership with mac kenya where they had incorporated some of their products on the platform. The credit model provided an accurate analysis and the platform is current incorporating electronic stores and african print products including fabrics, designer foot ware, bags and clothes to their catalogue.

Key Learning Points

Using machine learning I was able to analyse the customers purchasing trends and strategically place different products on the applications for different users to maximise on the users experience as they peruse the website. This enabled a boost sales and increase traffic to the applications.

Conclusion

Machine Learning and use of Artificial Intelligence is key in today's business success. Having used Machine Learning on this project to boost traffic I was amazed by the output before and after the implementation of the model. I have written a skeleton key model for similar projects that I may handle in future and continue iterating on it for maximum performance.
