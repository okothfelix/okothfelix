FAQS on Software Development

How long does it take to develop?

Depending on the project size, I might be able to get your MVP delivered in 2 weeks – 4 months. Subsequently, I can ensure releases of new functionality every 2–6 weeks. Get in touch, and we'll work out a way to meet your needs.

How much does it cost?

software development costs for a medium/large application may range from Kshs. 50K to Kshs. 300K. The total amount mainly depends on the number and complexity of software features, the number of platforms supported (web, mobile, desktop), and the type of development (custom, low-code, or platform-based). The user interface design uniqueness and complexity, the number of integrations with other software systems, as well as availability, security, and performance requirements also play an important part.

You can request free project cost calculation from us. Contact us today.

What’s your post-launch policy?

During the first 1–3 months after the software launch, I shall:

- Answers your and users’ questions, resolves user issues (if any).

- Manages incidents, configuration changes, and updates.

When the warranty support is over, upon agreement, I am ready to offer continuous maintenance and evolution of your software, as well as Level 1 - Level 3 support (if required).

What is your preferred development methodology?

For most of our software projects, I use Scrum with 2–3 week iterations. For small and mid-size projects with clearly defined and unchanging requirements, projects requiring formal approvals and projects with strict compliance requirements, I can opt for Waterfall.

Can you cover end-to-end software delivery?

Absolutely: I take charge of everything from requirements elicitation and software design to coding, deployment, and support.

How do you check the quality of the software you deliver?

I take a shift-left approach to QA, follow international coding standards, and maintain a quality management system at the level required by ISO 9001.
